$$TITLE$$="Customizing Your Spawners (in Survival)"\n
&lStep One&r: Create a tempered egg by smelting an egg (or Attuned Egg).\n
&lStep Two&r: Create an infused egg (enchanted book could have any enchantment and the egg in the middle is a tempered egg).\n
&lStep Three&r: Left-Click a mob with the infused egg to get an Attuned Egg with the given mob.\n
&lStep Four&r: Right-Click on a mob spawner with the Attuned Egg to change the mob it spawns!\n
